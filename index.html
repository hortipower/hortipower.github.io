  • Plant-centric lighting for faster, better and healthier growth

    HortiPower™ is specifically made to support plant growth and development and create optimum conditions for growth. With HortiPower growers can grow faster, better and healthier. Through a dedicated range of solutions HortiPower helps you provide the right light at the right time at the right area.

  • Flexible Spectrum control

    Every plant and growth stage has different needs. To best support photosynthesis, root development germination or other process, different lighting settings may be used. HortiPower offers full spectrum and flexible spectrum fixtures so that each time you can choose the best lighting for your plants any time and at any stage, without having to change the hardware. You may also use pre-sets which makes it easy to use.

  • System and algorithms

    HortiPower is designed to integrate in tissue culture shelves, city farming, floriculture greenhouses and greenwalls. With a complete set of accessories the HortiPower system is plug and play. With our own research team, we developed algorithms for several plants, so you may get a headstart. We also support research by universities and research labs.

  • Professional horticulture

    Designed for professional applications, HortiPower brings quality of light with extended lifetime, maximum efficiency and high output. All fixtures can be used in professional horticulture lighting design.

  • HortiPower Smart Spectrum Meter

    Power to the grower. Knowledge is power. So we created a smart spectrum meter just for you. It’s equipped with the most sensitive sensors for plant-centric light, along with fast processing ability, memory, and an easy to use design. For everyone from growers to scientists to lighting designers to engineers, HortiPower smart spectrum meter is ready to help you optimise your lights and grow faster, better and healthier.

  • Tissue Culture

    Speed up tissue culture growth and increase efficiency with HortiPower. HortiPower Tissue Culture is a cost effective direct view linear LED lighting fixture designed for tissue culture. Lighting properties such as intensity is adjustable to meet the tissue culture need at every growth stage. The special optical design supports an uniform lighting distribution on the shelf centre and edges. HortiPower is the world’s first full-spectrum and flexible spectrum fixture with custom engineered LEDs designed to support plant shooting, rooting and multiplication in tissue culture. Compared to traditional lighting it can save up to 50% energy costs as well as reduce maintenance cost.

  • Floriculture

    Advance or delay flowering time and manage fruiting with HortiPower Floriculture LED. HortiPower LED Floriculture is a cost-effective direct view and point-based LED lighting fixture designed for floriculture. HortiPower Floriculture is ideal for floriculture applications in the ornamental plants and flowering industry. Suitable for Cut flowers, Bedding plants, Perennials, Strawberries, Chrysanthemum and Green Dragon Fruit. Special lighting properties enable the grower to advance or delay the flowering time and maximise yield at optimum plant height and season. Compared to traditional solutions it can cut over 50% energy costs and a further 25% by smarter flowering time management.

  • GreenWall

    Feel well and bring the living wall alive with HortiPower Greenwall LED. HortiPower LED GreenWall is a cost-effective LED lighting fixture designed to maintain plant health and enhance the visual experience of a GreenWall.
